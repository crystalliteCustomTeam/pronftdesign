Weekly Update V 0.9.3

Author: Arham

This week in our weekly update we have fixed some old issues and brought in some new features. What are they? let’s find out.

What’s New:

  • Data extractions from Smart contracts:

To access essential metrics data immediately, users can provide their smart contract details to the app. Within minutes of data synchronization, Spock will present limited dApp data, which will be useful for the dApp founder to analyze initial data sets. However, for complete, comprehensive data availability and visualization, users will need to integrate SDK and code adapters.

  • Custom Pricing & Billing Mechanism:

We’ve made some changes to make it easier for you to pay as you need. Our freemium version lets you track up to 500 wallets for free, and if you need more, our Voyager version offers custom plans starting at $25. Plus, you can pay in stable cryptocurrencies like USDT, DAI, and USDC.

  • Tracking of Monthly tracked wallets on your dashboard:

Users can now track a monthly quota of MTW (Monthly Tracked Wallets) and view detailed information about their resource utilization. This feature provides users greater control over their analytics needs, allowing them to upgrade or downgrade their plan based on their tracking data. We hope this new feature will help you better manage your resources and get the insights you need to drive your dApp’s growth.

Bug Fixes:

  • Improvement in onboarding mechanism.
  • UX & Content changes in app.
  • Database management and improvements..

What’s Next:

  • The engineering team is working on V1 of Spock Analytics which consists of complete customizability of the complete dashboard and metrics.
